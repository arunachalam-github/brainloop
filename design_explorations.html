1 — Pulse Rings
Concentric rings expand outward like a heartbeat. Calm = slow single pulse · Busy = faster double · Chaotic = rapid overlapping bursts
2 — Particle Scatter
Dots scatter from center. Calm = few drifting slowly · Chaotic = many flying fast in all directions
3 — Waveform Bars
Vertical bars oscillate like an audio equalizer. Height and speed scale with switch count
4 — Ink Drop
A shape that morphs with cognitive load. Calm = smooth circle · Chaotic = distorted jagged blob
5 — Thread Tangle
Lines cross through each bucket. Calm = 1–2 straight lines · Chaotic = many tangled crossing lines that animate
6 — Flame / Fire
A flame per bucket. Calm = small steady flicker · Busy = taller flame · Chaotic = wild multi-tongue fire
7 — ECG / Heartbeat Line
A single continuous line across all buckets. Flat when empty · Gentle waves when calm · Sharp erratic spikes when chaotic
8 — Gravity Well
Dots orbit a center point. Calm = wide slow orbit · Chaotic = tight fast spiral, many dots pulled inward
9 — Breathing Circle
A circle that expands and contracts like breathing. Calm = slow deep breaths · Chaotic = rapid shallow hyperventilation with shake
10 — Sand Timer
Particles fall through an hourglass shape. Calm = slow trickle · Chaotic = flood of grains pouring fast
11 — Brainwave
Multiple EEG-style lines per bucket. Calm = smooth slow waves · Busy = tighter faster waves starting to tangle · Chaotic = wildly twisted knotted brainwave threads crossing each other
12 — Seismograph
One continuous line across all time. Flat when idle · Gentle undulation when calm · Tight dense vertical bursts when chaotic — like a seismic event or brainwave spike
